Modal

Modals are pop-ups that are used to show important information to the user. They are positioned over everything else in the document and remove scroll from the page.
Modals are different from Dialog Box as the user can't continue working on site until user responds to the Modal.
While in case of Dialog Box, user can continue working on site without necessarily interacting with it.
